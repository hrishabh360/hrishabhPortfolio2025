About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I am Hrishabh, a passionate Data Scientist and Software Developer with a keen interest in leveraging data to drive insights and build innovative solutions. My journey in technology has been shaped by a blend of academic excellence and hands-on experience in software development, data engineering, and web technologies.

Professionally, I have contributed to impactful projects such as the Nepal Stock Exchange (NEPSE) system, where I served as both a Software Development Engineer in Test and a Full-Stack Developer. My responsibilities included developing and testing core modules of the trading and fund management systems, enhancing software performance, and ensuring product reliability. I worked closely with cross-functional teams to implement features across the front-end and back-end, leveraging tools such as Java, Oracle, and modern web technologies to meet business and regulatory requirements.

I am passionate about leveraging data science to build scalable solutions and uncover meaningful insights. Open to exciting collaborations and opportunities, I aim to continuously grow as a problem-solver and contribute to innovations at the intersection of AI, analytics, and software engineering.
